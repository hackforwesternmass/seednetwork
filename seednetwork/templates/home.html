The Hilltown Seed Saving Network is a group of people in the Hilltowns of Western Massachusetts interested in saving seeds. We are working to educate ourselves and our neighbors about seed saving. Our virtual seed bank is for sharing information about what seeds we have in our own collections and how best to grow them in this region. We are from Cummington, Chesterfield, Plainfield, Worthington, Windsor, Goshen, Ashfield and surrounding towns in the region and meet monthly in Cummington. More information about our group can be found at http://hilltownseeds.wordpress.com/. Please join us.
